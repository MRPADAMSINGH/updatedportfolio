Features:

  • Information about all the places.
  • Booking system for Flight and Hotel.
  • Dresses and Photos of Indian culture.
  • User can contact us through Email and social media handles.

Mrs. Culture, an inspiring college project focused on travel and Indian culture, was successfully executed with the expertise of Project Team Leader Padam Singh and the invaluable assistance of Trainer Mala Mishra. Mala Mishra played a crucial role in providing guidance and support throughout the project's development. As a skilled trainer, she brought her knowledge and expertise in Indian culture, helping the team gain deeper insights and ensuring the project's content remained authentic and culturally accurate.

Mala Mishra's contributions in coordinating with local experts, content curation, and fostering an atmosphere of cultural appreciation were instrumental in shaping the project's success. Her dedication and commitment ensured that Mrs. Culture became a comprehensive and immersive platform, captivating users and leaving a lasting impact on the exploration of India's rich cultural heritage.
